About GSE College

GSE which stands for Graduate Self Employement is one of the programs that the University of Ilorin use to engage the students to ensure that each of them has at least an entrepreneurship skill asides the formal education received in the classroom. One of the skills under GSE is Web Development. Due to the advancement in technology, the rate at which students are now opting for web and software development is increasing on a daily basis. The rate at which software development skill is needed by companies keep on increasing yearly. Hence, there is need to train men who will fit for this role and most importantly who will be able to solve community problem using their codes.

GSE Web Development Class has grown from just being a course taken yearly by students into a full course or training which students can enroll in at any time. The course outline is flexible enough to ensure that students can combine it together with their studies in the school. There are also some advantages that come along with it. Some of them include:
  1. Exposure to new technologies
  2. Learning from senior sofware engineers
  3. Internship opportunities and so on
